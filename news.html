Upcoming Film Role

Jennifer Garner has a major upcoming role in the movie Wonder Park. Wonder Park is an animated movie that is due for release in March of 2019. She will be starring alongside Brianna Denski, Ken Hudson Campbell, Kenan Thompson, Mila Kunis, and others.

Major role in new HBO show, Camping

Jennifer Garner has a major role starring alongside David Tennant in the new HBO show Camping, created by Lena Dunham and Jenni Konner. This is a show about a group of friends on a camping trip that spirals out of control.

Jennifer Garner & Ben Affleck Have Officially Divorced

Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck have officially finalized their divorce as of November of 2018. Unlike many other couples, a divorce lawyer was not necessary, however, the entire process was mediated by a lawyer. They did not have a pre-nup and decided to divide property evenly. They're sharing joint custody of their three children and are attending co-parenting therapy for six months.

Jennifer Garner Roasts Bradley Cooper

Jennifer Garner roasts former Alias co-star Bradley Cooper at the 32nd Annual Cinematheque Awards honoring Bradley Cooper. She was alongside Ed Helms, Sean Penn, and Zack Galifianakis.
